Recipe #1: Peanut butter and Jelly Sandwich


Things you will need...

Item Amount
Bread 2 slices
Knife 1
Peanut Butter 1 jar
Jelly 1 jar
  1. Place the two slices of bread on a plate
  2. Open the jar of jelly and the jar of peanut butter
  3. Take the knife in place it into one of the jars. Jelly is the better choice.
  4. Spread the covered knife onto one of the bread slices
  5. Place the knife into the other jar, making sure to blend the contents like a degenerate
  6. Spread the knife onto the other slice of bread
  7. Place the two slices together, with the peanut butter and jelly touching

Recipe #2: Honey/Banana/Peanut Butter Sandwich


Things you will need...

Item Amount
Bread 2 slices
Knife 1
Peanut Butter 1 jar
Banana 1
Honey 1 jar
  1. Place the two slices of bread on a plate
  2. Open the jar of jelly and the jar of peanut butter
  3. Take the knife in place it into the jar of peanut butter
  4. Spread the covered knife onto one of the bread slices
  5. Cut up a banana and line the pieces on top of the peanut butter
  6. Drizzle honey on top of the bananas and peanut butter
  7. Place the two slices together, with the banana side in the center
